The Resilient Societies Initiative

The Resilience Development Initiative supports COVID-19-response efforts that create long-term civilizational resilience. We believe resilience infrastructure can include both immediate practical needs, such as consolidating PPE donation efforts and building contact-tracing systems, and long-term cultural shifts. For projects developing this infrastructure, we offer funding, talent, and connections to an extensive network of professionals responding to the crisis.

If you know of such a project, or would like to contribute resources to our initiative, please contact us at contact@resilientsocietiesinitiative.org.
